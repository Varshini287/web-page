Our Business
Our core business area is low-power AC drives.
From concept to production, our product is our baby. We have in-house resource to address each stage of product development - product description, hardware & software design, development, validation, production & testing. Thus we are better positioned to cut total cost of ownership﻿, even as we add more features and better service. Our strength lies in prompt nationwide service.
We also render consultancy service. In this regard, we are glad to inform that we are the indepent design house for NXP (Freescale Semiconductors) and Arrow Electronics Inc.

Our Services

    Hexmoto undertakes consultancy projects in the field of motor control,
    power supplies and industrial automation. Our current area of focus
    is high frequency UPS, solar hybrid power supply and industrial automation.


  • Service 1

    • Solar Power

    Hexmoto has taken cognizance of the importance of sustainable development.
    The need of the hour is to evolve practical means to harness the abundant solar energy .
    In this direction, we have delivered a solar inverter to drive agricultural pumps and home lighting.
    More designs for industrial and domestic use are in the pipeline.



  • Service 2

    • Power supply

    Hexmoto has delivered custom made DC power supplies with ratings upto 60 kW with proven
    short circuit protection and stiff voltage regulation.
    High frequency UPS designs are in the offing.

  • Service 3

    • Motor Control

    Custom design of high speed motor controllers with:

    1. Closed-loop voltage and current control
    2. Custom specific switching technique
    3.Low THD
    4. Short circuit protection and various fault monitoring
    5. Custom designed human machine interface(HMI).
