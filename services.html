CONSULTANCY


Hexmoto undertakes consultancy projects in the field of motor control, power supplies and industrial automation.Our current area of focus is high frequency UPS, solar hybrid power supply and industrial automation.

  • Solar Power

    Hexmoto has delivered custom made DC power supplies with ratings upto 60 kW with proven short circuit protection and stiff voltage regulation. High frequency UPS designs are in the offing.

  • Power supply

    Hexmoto has taken cognizance of the importance of sustainable development.The need of the hour is to evolve practical means to harness the abundant solar energy .In this direction, we have delivered a solar inverter to drive agricultural pumps and home lighting. More designs for industrial and domestic use are in the pipeline.

  • Motor Control

    Custom design of high speed motor controllers with:

    1. Closed-loop voltage and current control
    2. Custom specific switching technique
    3.Low THD
    4. Short circuit protection and various fault monitoring
    5. Custom designed human machine interface(HMI).
